The Groove Lounge

Project Site Plan Document

Site Name

The Groove Lounge

This name was selected because:

  • Represents the musical focus ("Groove")
  • Conveys a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere ("Lounge")
  • Easy to remember and brandable
  • Domain availability: groovelounge.com (preferred)

Site Purpose

The Groove Lounge website will serve as:

  • A digital hub for our local music venue
  • Primary source for upcoming event information
  • Platform for ticket sales and reservations
  • Resource for artists looking to perform
  • Information center for venue details and policies

Scenarios

Target audience questions the site will answer:

  1. Music Fan: "What live performances are happening this weekend at The Groove Lounge?"
  2. Local Artist: "How can I book a performance slot at this venue?"
  3. First-time Visitor: "What's the dress code and age policy for your events?"
  4. Event Planner: "Can I rent the venue for a private event?"
